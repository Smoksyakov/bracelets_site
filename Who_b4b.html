We know what you're probably thinking: why would I spend my money to buy these bracelets? Well the answer is that for every braclet purchased, 10% is going to one of the three charities we work with. We want to donate to charities who we believe support a rightful cause of helping animals who can't help themselves.
Every purchase raises a minimum of a dollar to helping the three foundations we work with: African Wildlife Foundation, Save the Elephants and Animal Welfare Institue. These organisations do not focus on stealing your donations, but instead put every cent towards a better and safer life for most wildlife animals who are in danger of poachers.
Help animals who can't help themselves. We can all work together and build a proper environment for all animals to live and enjoy their lives in.
With all proceeds collected, every bracelet helps Charities to do the right thing. Poaching is increasingly killing off the population of animals, but we can fight back
For animals, any effort counts. One person can make a huge difference. One dollar can make a huge difference. But the biggest difference is when we work Together
